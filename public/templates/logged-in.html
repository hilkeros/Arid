Exclusive brunch and prelistening of the new album

21 December 0

You want the privilege to hear the new album before everyone else? You want to listen to the music while having a chat with the band members and enjoying a delicious brunch? And you want to support a good cause with all that? Well, you can do all of that if you bid on the auction that we set up together with Studio Brussel and Music For Life. On February 14th (yes, Valentine's day!) 20 people will have the exclusive right to attend a brunch and hear the new songs of the upcoming album in the presence of the band members. You can make a bid here.
